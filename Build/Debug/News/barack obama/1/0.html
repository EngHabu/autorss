President Barack Obama makes burritos for soup kitchen charity. President Barack Obama honoured Martin Luther King Jr's legacy of service on Monday by helping a soup kitchen prepare its daily meals.President Obama took his wife, Michelle, and daughters Malia and Sasha to DC Central Kitchen, which is a few minutes away from the White House by presidential motorcade.They joined an assembly line that was churning out burritos.Asked what the burritos were being stuffed with, President Obama said it looked like lamb. It actually was beef in a sauce, along with unidentified vegetables and cheese.
